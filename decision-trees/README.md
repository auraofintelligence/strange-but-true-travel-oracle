## Decision Rule

A strong destination needs all of these:

- safe enough
- legal enough
- financially survivable
- logistically possible
- useful under the Strange But True banner
- respectful of Shared Table, food, hospitality, and cultural context
- private risk understood

Pattern notes can influence what to check next, but they do not replace the checks.
