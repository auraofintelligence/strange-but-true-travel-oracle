# Oracle Score

Use this score as a thinking aid, not a command.

## Score Bands

- 0-20: no-go
- 21-40: weak
- 41-60: watch
- 61-80: candidate
- 81-100: strong candidate

## Categories

| Category | Points |
| --- | ---: |
| Safety and consular viability | 20 |
| Visa and legal feasibility | 15 |
| Budget and route feasibility | 15 |
| Serendipity and spontaneity | 10 |
| Romance and intercultural relationships | 10 |
| Strange But True fit | 10 |
| Shared Table and cultural context | 10 |
| Timing and recovery | 10 |
| Contacts and opportunity | 5 |
| Pattern/planning signal | 10 |

## Pattern Signal Rules

Pattern signal can add attention, not override danger.

Give high pattern points only when the signal produces a practical check or useful timing caution.

## Minimum Gates

Even with a high total score:

- safety below 8/20 means no-go or watch
- visa below 6/15 means prepare only
- budget below 6/15 means pause
- romance/intercultural context below 4/10 means do not treat relationship possibility as a reason to move
- Shared Table/cultural context below 4/10 means do not publish any table story
