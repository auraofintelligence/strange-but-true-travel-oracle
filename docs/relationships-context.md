# Relationships Context

This repo treats romance and intercultural relationships as part of real travel.

It also keeps a private link to the Global Group Marriages / Love U.N. frame.

Public context:

https://globalgroupmarriages.com

Private source notes:

- `GGM_Philosophising.md`
- `GGM_Marriage_statistics.md`

## What This Layer Holds

- romance
- attraction
- intercultural curiosity
- dating and relationship norms
- family, kinship, and marriage patterns
- consent and boundaries
- legal and cultural caution
- language and communication
- non-monogamy or group-family ideas where relevant
- Love U.N. / Global Group Marriages as speculative context

## What This Layer Refuses

This is not:

- a pickup strategy
- a dating funnel
- a KPI for travel
- a way to turn people into content
- a claim that real people consent to a theory

## GGM Lens

Use GGM as a private philosophical lens for:

- how love scales
- how families mutate under global mobility
- how consent and care could become governance
- how intercultural relationships shift identity
- how law lags behind lived relational forms
- how AI could reflect, mediate, or distort intimacy

Do not use GGM as a script for real encounters.

## Required Questions

1. What is actually present, not imagined?
2. What is legal here?
3. What is culturally respectful here?
4. Are language, money, visa status, age, gender, status, or public visibility creating vulnerability?
5. What should stay private?
6. Does the GGM frame illuminate the moment, or does it overtake it?

## Agent Rule

People are not content, proof, tokens, or project resources.
