Real-time checks and future-sensing stay separate.

The planning layer can hold intuition and precognition-style notes, but every signal needs a practical check or a reflection-only label.

Planning Lanes

Each lane has a different evidence level.

Official

Action evidence

Government, embassy, insurer, airline, health, legal, or primary sources.

Operational

Practical evidence

Routes, accommodation, costs, contacts, local timing, weather, and event logistics.

Pattern

Private signal

Dreams, intuition, repeated symbols, body signals, and scenario rehearsals.
