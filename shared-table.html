The Travel Pattern

Do not copy the Straddie model everywhere. Use it as a respectful way to notice how each place gathers and cares.

Gather

Where people meet

Markets, kitchens, halls, temples, cafes, shelters, hostels, gardens, farms, and family tables.

Check

Permission and safety

Food safety, cultural protocol, photography consent, local law, and whether a visitor should stay quiet.

Share

Only where invited

Reciprocity first. Public story only after privacy, consent, and cultural context are clear.
