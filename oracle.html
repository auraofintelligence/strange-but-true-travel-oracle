Decision Bands

Plain labels make it easier for agents to hand off without hiding behind clever words.

Go

Ready enough

Safety, entry, money, arrival, recovery, and privacy checks are acceptable.

Prepare

Promising but unfinished

No hard stop, but one or two practical checks still matter.

Watch

Timing may change

Useful signal, but safety, cost, route, or invitation timing is unstable.

Pause

Slow the decision

Emotion, health, budget, or source freshness needs attention first.

No-go

Hard stop

Official risk, legal path, budget, or privacy exposure makes the move wrong for now.

Private

Keep internal

Any live route, personal signal, or sensitive dossier stays out of public channels.
